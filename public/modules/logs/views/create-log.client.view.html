Logs are, additional, snapshot-like descriptions of an experience. Sometimes it's not always clear which experience it is related to, though, and that's okay!
An example log might be something like this:
    I decided to listen to her.
      I felt very accomplished for the things I said.
        I learned that we are both very opimistic of the future.
          I cared about her.
